Detailed Description

template<typename T, size_t N, StnType STN, typename Problem, size_t I, BCF ... P>
class Uintah::PhaseField::detail::bcfd_view< VectorField< T, N >, STN, Problem, index_sequence< I >, P... >

Wrapper of DataWarehouse variables for both basic and complex differential operations over both physical and amr boundaries (VectorField implementation)

Adds to dw_view the possibility to compute finite-difference approximation of of differential operations at boundary cells/points

Template Parameters
Problemtype of PhaseField problem
Ivariable index within Problem
Plist of BC, FC, and Patch::Face packs
